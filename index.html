Simple Promodoro Timer

  • A Pomodoro timer is a time management tool that enables you to concentrate on what you're doing for a certain period of time, usually 25 minutes, before taking a little break of about 5 minutes.
  • It is a technique for boosting productivity by dividing work into brief, concentrated periods.
  • You may enhance your focus and productivity by avoiding distractions, managing your time better by using a Pomodoro timer.
